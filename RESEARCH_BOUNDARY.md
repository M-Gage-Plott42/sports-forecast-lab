# Research Boundary

Sports Forecast Lab is a public research framework. Its purpose is to
demonstrate source review, data contracts, validation, model documentation, and
cross-sport adapter design.

## Included

- Public-source sample data.
- Synthetic demo rows.
- Model-card and dataset-card examples.
- Public export validation.
- Fixture-only MLB adapter scaffolding.

## Excluded

- Private ledgers and private account records.
- App/slate records, screenshots, and transaction details.
- Generated bulk datasets, caches, and model binaries.
- Secrets, tokens, credentials, and local filesystem paths.
- Account automation, live recommendations, and financial instructions.

## Standard

Every public source row should preserve source/access status, timestamps when
available, and a review note. Model claims require a dataset card, model card,
baseline comparison, calibration checks, leakage review, and explicit
promotion status.
